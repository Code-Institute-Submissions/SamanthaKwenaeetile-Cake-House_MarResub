About us

Welcome! We are a family run business specialising in cakes and cupcakes. We bake and decorate everything on site in our cosy little corner of east Belfast on Bloomfield Avenue. Whether it is for a gift, a big party or a sneaky treat our cupcakes and cakes fit the bill for any occasion. Each box goes out with love, care and a big dollop of yum for your tum!
